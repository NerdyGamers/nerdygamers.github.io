About Us

 NerdyGamers
The home of free Plugins, Scripts, and Coding Help for a variety of games.

NerdyGamers was created with a single mission: to produce gamer server plugins/scripts for everybody to use and learn from. All of the plugins on this websites Unturned plugins use RocketMod. All of our Plugins/Scripts are free. The majority of the plugins on this website are created by a couple dudes named JBob and Ragnarok. This website is also maintained by the same nerds.

If you would like to contribute to development, please check out our GitHub. If you would like to buy the peoples named JBob and Ragnarok something nice, donate Here.
